The Case for the Platform

Why these numbers
tell a good story.

The baseline is under $190/month

The full platform — multi-school, AI-powered, secure, live — costs less per month than a tank of gas per school. That's the foundation the comms layer is built on top of.

Cost per school drops 70% at scale

Fixed costs are already paid at school one. Every school added after that primarily adds AI usage and communication volume — not new infrastructure. The economics improve as the portfolio grows.

The comms cost is tied to revenue

Every Twilio call and SMS is a rep touching a donor or ticket buyer. That activity drives conversions. The communications cost scales with outreach volume — which scales with revenue opportunity.

Adding a school takes one config entry

The platform is built to grow without rebuilding. New school, new branding, new data — one configuration entry. No new deployments, no new infrastructure, no new engineering work required.
